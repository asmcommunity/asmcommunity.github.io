Guys,

lets not beat around the bush on the distinction between reverse engineering software and cracking.

Reverse engineering has its basis in doing very unglamourous stuff like old mainframe code where the source was on punchcards and the owner of the software wants it ported to a PC or similar.

A lot of this stuff was done for the year 2k problem so that the planet did not stop rotating. :)

Cracking disguised as reverse engineering is another matter. Decompiling code for the purpose of breaking its protection system is just plain illegal in many countries and it is not allowed in this forum no matter what.

Our moderators have eagle eyes when it comes to any of this stuff being posted and it will head for the scrapheap REAL fast if it is posted here. Anyone who persists with posting this stuff here will be given the opportunity of finding another forum that is more tolerant. :tongue:
