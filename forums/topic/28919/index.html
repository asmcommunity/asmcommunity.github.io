run "dxdiag",  see the "Display" tab.

Open your PC's case and look inside lol.
But a quick way to see if your videocard is onboard without even turning the PC on or opening it - is to look at the back panel. If the VGA cable is near the USB/PS2/LPT, then it's integrated. Otherwise, the VGA cable is on one of the PCI slits.
