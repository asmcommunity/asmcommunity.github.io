Hi, I know your problem. I had the same problem when I made a prog ('Window Shutdown') which stays in the taskbar to shutdown computer or reboot or disconnect. -->When you exit the prog, you must 'delete' the icon from the taskbar : just a few lines forward, you see that : ;;;;;;;;;;; invoke WinMain,hInstance,NULL,CommandLine,SW_SHOWDEFAULT invoke Shell_NotifyIcon,NIM_DELETE,addr note ;<-- YOU MUST ADD THIS HERE TO DELETE THE ICON invoke ExitProcess,NULL ;;;;;;;;;;; You can also put this line in the WM_DESTROY or WM_CLOSE message. Hope this will help you.
