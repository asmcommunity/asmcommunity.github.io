link problems
You can try this:

ml /c /coff /Cp test.asm
rc rsrc.rc
cvtres /machine:ix86 rsrc.res /out:rsrc.obj
link /SUBSYSTEM:WINDOWS /libpath:c:\masm32\lib test.obj rsrc.obj

Or you can add \masm23\bin to your path in autoexec.bat (if you have Win9X).
