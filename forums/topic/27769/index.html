Changing A Dialogs Titlebar
The caption color and those buttons are all part of the 'non-client' area. While you can work there yourself intercepting the weird "NC" messages, it can be a draining expierance trying to track them all down. Personally, I gave up and used an easier way. I defined a region as large as the client area of my window, then applied that. This masks off the caption, buttons, etc. I found it easier to re-create them they to try to change the existing ones. My tut on this is Custom Captions (and shapes too).
