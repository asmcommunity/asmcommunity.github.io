well the only way i could think of how to do that is like this: 


.data
good  db "Good Morning Vietnam",13,10,0 
wasup db "Wassup?",13,10,0" 

.data?
allone dd ?


.code
invoke lstrcat,allone,good
invoke lstrcat,allone,wasup

;rest of code here to put it back in
thats my go at it, hope it helps. -brad
