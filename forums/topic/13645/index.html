relations were not so evidently pushy, but this time?

don't get me wrong i like pop-art cinema but there are movies that are SO damn
much better than THIS - you already saw a few of david lynchs films? lost
highway, mullholland-drive, blue velvet? you'll find dozens of hidden allusions
but you really can tell by watching it that it's definitively not made to make a
shitload of money. another thing is the stagecraft of the actors, i can not
comprehend why they tried to reduce emotions to a minimum, everybody in
this movie looks so terribly dull. and those intruding pseudo-wisdoms, haha
these are taken straight out of philosophy 101 - it's ridiculous. god damn, i
rather read a good book instead of going throught that limp again next
november.

that's what i think about that movie, so i know for a fact that many many people
liked it very much and i also know for a fact that quantity doesn't relate to quality
in any way but i'm really interested what YOU think. am i completely wrong? so
please "enlighten" me, what's so great and special (and i DON'T mean the special-
effects with this) about matrix-reloaded?

oh one last thing. you definitively can not disagree here, the soundtrack is not
worth a single listen (maybe except ratm). but it's obvious why the choosed
"nu-metal" as matrix's musical-layer - shallow entertainment for the ma$$es.
funny how the pieces fit - wow, i solved the matrix.
