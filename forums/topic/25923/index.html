Re: help,about AAM
aam, aad,..., are(were) undocumented opcodes (in their full form), therefore are not supported in masm.
however, there is a simple workaround (instead of ugly hardcoded bytes):
option nokeyword:<aad,aam>
aad macro i:=<10d>
db 0d5h,i
endm
aam macro i:=<10d>
db 0d4h,i
endm
