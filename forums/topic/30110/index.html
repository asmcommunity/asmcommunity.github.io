Hi - there's a good chance that you've inadvertently disabled a dependency.

Go to the Services applet (control panel) and find the Fax service you mentioned.
Stop the service.
Now get properties and enumerate the list of Dependency services.
Finally, make sure ALL the services which it depends apon are running.
Restart the service, and check whether the self-start method is appropriate.
