Long message? Looks like a fairly short message to me. ;)
Do you mean print as in send text to a printer? Get a device context for the printer and use APIs such as TextOut() to write text to it.
Do you mean print as in display text on the screen? Same as above, but use a display DC. Or you could always go the easy route and use MessageBox()
Or maybe you mean print as in send text to the console? Look up the WriteConsole() and other such functions in the PSDK.
