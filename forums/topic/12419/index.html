new job :)
Write a program that does something useful. Doesn't have to be a word processor but could be a sophisticated timer or something. Then call a company and ask for the name of the Engineering Department's manager name. Send him a copy of it and Human Resources (if they have one). The smaller the company, the better. Then tell them you'll be taking Computer Science or something in college when you get out of high school and you'd really like the chance at an internship at minimum wage (or free if you can). If you can work somewhere like that for the summer, you're in! You've got a resume started. Now you can aim higher. At least if you get to do some coding at that summer job.
