Papers of the OOPSLA conferences
The "Proceedings" are published by the ACM. You can buy them from ACM. My experience with the e-store was not very good (too slow). It would be more pleasant to order it through a university book store.

The full title for the 2002 Conference is Proceedings of the 17th ACM conference on Object-oriented programming, systems, languages, and applications.

Each of the papers in the proceedings are also available for download from the ACM's Digital Library. However, this requires ACM membership plus subscription to the Digital Library service. Fortunately, you don't need membership to view the titles of the papers.
