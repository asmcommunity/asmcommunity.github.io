Hi LaptoniC

It is the MakeDef.dll addin that fools you.
Open RadASM.ini and change the line in section .

Change from:
xx=MakeDef.dll,2

To:
xx=MakeDef.dll,1

This makes MakDef to output exports to outputwindow instead of directly to the file.
