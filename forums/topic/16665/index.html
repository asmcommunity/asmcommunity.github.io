MiniWeb 0.7 - plug-ins enabled
I've made some changes to my MiniWeb server, and designed a plug-in architecture, so that functions of CGI and CGI-PRO is provided by their respective plugins. In this way, they can be easily removed from MiniWeb just by removing the respective plugins. And also new functions can be adopted by writing new plugins.

Download: http://member.netease.com/~huangyc/miniweb/
