TBPaint Addin
For those of you who use my TBPaint package, this is an addin that will integrate it into RadASM. It adds a item to the context menu in the Project browser. When you right click on a bitmap you can select "Open with Toolbar Edit" and it will open your toolbar graphic directly in TBPaint. You must copy TBPaint to the RadASM\Addins folder as well as the addin's DLL. Enable the addin in the normal way, it is AddIn Manager compatible.
