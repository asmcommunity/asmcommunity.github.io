GPA help
Im my university it was on a weighed course/grade average.

For instance:

Basket weaving -- Challenge Level 2 -- Grade A = (11/12)
RF Lines and Antennas -- Challenge Level 5 -- Grade B- (7/12)

Weighted Averages:

Basket Weaving [ 2 * 11 = 22 ]
RF Lines & Ants [ 5 * 7 = 35 ]
------------------------------------------
Total course wieghts = 2+5 = 7
Total course grades = 22 + 35 = 57

GPA = 57 / 7 = 8.14 / 12

Our grade system was:

A+ = 12. A = 11. A- = 10.
B+ = 9. B = 8. B- = 7.
C+ = 6. C = 5. C- = 4.
D+ = 3. D = 2. D- = 1.

All other letters are 0. (However 'D' often to on an alternate "D is for Diploma" point of view ;) )
