I would not be disappointed by that. Probably all the materials can be downloaded from ON Semi's website.

Although here in Canada I can get printed materials and CD's, I never order them.
Moreover, when suppliers offer free CD's or printed materials I generally refuse them. The reason is that the most up to date information is on the web (and it does not take up space on my desk).

It is not uncommon to use a master selection CD and later learn that there is newer, better component available, which was not included when the CD was made. Today, a lot of things change over a six month period, so the catalog you ordered yesterday may be incomplete tomorrow.

Just download everything you need from the websites of interest. And you don't need to wait for the catalog to arrive.
