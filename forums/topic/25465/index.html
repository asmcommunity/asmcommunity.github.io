Windows LIVE Messenger (MSN) Login Problems
Heya all..

I've had big problems logging certain accounts into MSN using their latest client for the past two weeks or more, and I'm not alone.
I tried all the solutions I could find, for example re-registering all the COMponents, uninstalling and reinstalling the client, nothing worked until today.

?The solution was to delete the registry keys from HKCU\Software\Microsoft\MsnMessenger downwards.
This won't cause any harm, those keys will be recreated next time you run time client and login to your account(s).

Have a nice day :)
