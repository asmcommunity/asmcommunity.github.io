  you could also Rotate or shift but it seems TEST is the most appropriate.

  test ax,1000000000000000b
  jz its_zero
its_one:
  do stuff for one
  jmp somwhere
its_zero
  do stuff for one

  or

  shl/rcl ax,1
  jnz/jnc it_zero
its_one:
  do stuff for one
  jmp somwhere
its_zero:
  do stuff for zero
