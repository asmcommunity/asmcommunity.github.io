The triangle one is due to the fact that it isn't a triangle ;)

If you check the slope of the red triangle, it is 3 units y over 8 units x = 0.375

The slope of the green one is 2 units y over 5 units x = 0.4

So when put together they don't even form a straight line, although it is close enough to fool you at first glance. If you look carefully you can kinda see how the big triangle "caves in" in the first example and "bulges out" in the second at the hypotenuse. The extra square went into this "bulge"
