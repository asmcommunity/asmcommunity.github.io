Damn! It's my turn to cook again?

"Pork chop - Salsa":

Cut pork chops into slices of about 3/4" width... fry them 'til they're slighly brown...
Slap a whole jar of the mild/spicy salsa (the kind you use to dip chips in) into the frying pan...
And let this all simmer for about 15 minutes... Use those 15 minutes to make some
instant mashed potatoes...and a vegetable to make this all healthy.

Always a hit...
