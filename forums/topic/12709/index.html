RadASM suggestion
There are left and right arrows that appear on the tab bar to scroll it when there are too many tabs to display at once (small buttons on the far right of the tab bar). If you want them all to be displayed at once you can select "Tool Windows" from the "options" menu and enable multiline tabs then adjust the height of the tab bar to fit all of the tabs.
