Re: i need helpe
I assume you mean FAT as in FAT12/16/32/VFAT, the old filesystem that has been superceded by NTFS?

Assuming you're going to do this from a Win32 application, the first step is deciding whether you want Win9x support, or only need to handle NT. For NT, direct disk access is easy, it's done with CreateFile using a special "filename" - all documented in the MSDN/PlatformSDK. Win9x is more complicated.

Then, there's the issue of reading/parsing the FAT. For this, you'll need a reference of the FAT system. Have a look at http://www.nondot.org/sabre/os/articles/FileSystems/ .

Once you get started, I'm sure people here are willing to answer more specific questions :-)
