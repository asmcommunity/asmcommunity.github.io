When somebody shares server resource in Win9x (with NetShareAdd or manually) in registry HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Network\LanMan\
apears a value Flags=0x192 (or 101h or 302h and so on).
As I understand it is a value of flags, but I know only:
SHI50F_RDONLY equ 01h
SHI50F_FULL equ 02h
SHI50F_DEPENDSON equ SHI50F_RDONLY or SHI50F_FULL
SHI50F_ACCESSMASK equ SHI50F_RDONLY or SHI50F_FULL
SHI50F_PERSIST equ 100h ; The share is restored on system startup
SHI50F_SYSTEM equ 200h ; The share is not normally visible

302h=SHI50F_FULL or SHI50F_SYSTEM or SHI50F_PERSIST
101h=SHI50F_RDONLY or SHI50F_PERSIST
