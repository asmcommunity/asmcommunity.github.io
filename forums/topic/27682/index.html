How to get text into splitted status bar ?
you only need to have the handle of the status bar put this into .IF uMsg==WM_CREATE invoke SendMessage,StatusBarHandle,SB_SETTEXT,1,ADDR text1 the 1 is the section of the statusbar you want text. the sections are numbered 0 through how many you have. change the number to whatever section you want your text. smurf
