sniffer
Hook the NDIS driver, grab all the packages, dump them, send them on their way, decode them... Simple as that... As for the coding... Can't help you there... It might be a good idea to really figure out what types of packages you wanna grab... If you only want TCP/IP you don't have to hook NDIS, it will be enough to hook the TCP/IP (tcpip.sys in NT/2K/XP as far as I know)... And so on... Good luck
