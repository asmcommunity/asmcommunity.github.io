PredeX,

The Microsoft PWBs came with DOS languages like MASM 6/6.1 and C v6 but they were always attached to a commercial package so there is no chance of finding a legal free copy of it.

They were a reasonable editor in their time but there are many better available now. Perhaps one of the Guys who is familiar with DOS editors may be able to recommend a legal free one for you.

The alternative is to find a second hand set of disks for MASM and install it to get the PWB.
