I made a board in Excel for this game. Just to organize things better. My conclusions are that the best strategy (for now) is to use only few cannons in the beginning (no arrows) to get the g bonus. Go with those until you reach about level 15 more or less (past the first boss at least, you will miss few lives in the beginning but its worth now than later) At this point, get water upgrade to slow down enemies, water towers at level 1 are ok. Save up on g.
When you get second upgrade, get fire. and spend only on them later on.

In fact the conclusions are:

You dont need:
Arrow towers [121% cost/ben]
neither Air towers [186% cost/ben, but used 3 times in game]
neither Earth towers
neither Rocket
You need:
About 4-5 water towers
About at least 5 fire towers at level 4
As many fire towers as possible whenever you can buy them later on.

Note:
I could arrive at level 25 with only 1 water tower and 1 fire tower at level 4 with few cannons for backup. My best level was 35 but now that I learnt the 'timing' I will try it again.  8)
