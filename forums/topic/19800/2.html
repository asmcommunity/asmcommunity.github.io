Pull ups
Pull-ups with knuckles facing you is more of a upper back excercise, biceps sure help a little, but it's still the back that counts. The width of the grip makes a little difference too, the correct grip should be a little wider than the shoulders. And of course the way you keep your legs, this affects on the center point of your mass, and therefor the muscles that keep you in place. I like to keep my legs bend.

I used to do pull-ups with extra weights on the belt, but haven't done those in a while. Sometimes i still do them without the extra weights, and i think i can make like 10 with a slow tempo, but let's keep in mind that i weight 105kg.. :P
