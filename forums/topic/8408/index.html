PAINTSTRUCT in win32.inc for TASM
The PAINTSTRUCT is declared incorrectly in win32.inc that is supplied with TASM. 



PAINTSTRUCT in win32.inc..



PAINTSTRUCT STRUC

    PShdc   UINT		   ?

    PSfErase	  UINT		   ?

    PSrcPaint	  UCHAR 	   size RECT dup(?)

    PSfRestore	  UINT		   ?

    PSfIncUpdate  UINT		   ?

    PSrgbReserved UCHAR 	   16 dup(?)

PAINTSTRUCT ENDS


The PSrgbReserved member should be 32 dup(?)

Just wanted to let everyone know.
