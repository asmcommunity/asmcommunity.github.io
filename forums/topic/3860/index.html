Hi franlou

To change a property you first have to click on the line in the listbox and then click again on the name.

(Name) | IDC_STC

1. Click on IDC_STC to make a selection in the listbox.
2. Click on IDC_STC again to enter the edit control.

You can also use the keyboard.
Shift+Ctrl+Up/Down make a selection in the listbox.
Shift+Ctrl+Right enter the edit control or show the sub listbox.
