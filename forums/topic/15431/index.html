Melt 1.0.2
Forgotten what I worked on last when I moved in the beginning of the month, but did work on a few things and just tested this and all seems to work.

Install:
Unzip to your WinASM addins directory Then use the addin manager to setup. When you create a note a subdirectory will be created (MELT Notes).

Uninstall:
Delete the subdirectory MELT Notes and the following dlls in the addins directory:
CVTRES.DLL
LINKLIB.DLL
ML.DLL
NMAKE.DLL
RCC.DLL
RCW.DLL
meltwa.dll
