QDToA???
The DIV instruction should work for you. Put the 64-bit number in EDX:EAX and divide by 1,000,000,000 (the largest power of ten that fits into 32-bits). Call DWTOA with EAX. Call DWTOA with EDX. Put the two strings together - padding the second string to nine digits. Of course this has the limitation of the 64-bit number being between zero and 2^31 * 1,000,000,000 - 1.
