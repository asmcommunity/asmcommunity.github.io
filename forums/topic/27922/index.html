The fractal itself is called the sierpinski triangle. I have written a dos program (in assembly of course) that draws the triangle. I used the pascal triangle to draw it, not the 'random'-method, but without calculating the numbers. This is not necessary as you can predict if a number will be odd or even: odd+odd = always even, even+even= always even, even+odd/odd+even = always odd. This exacly matches a XOR operation.
