Win32 nasm bitmap fader I made
This was part of an old project of mine in 12th grade to develop a
Win32 screensaver. It will load a file specified by the BitmapPath
variable and simply fade it in and out until you kill the program.
Being that I'm not using OpenGL or DirectX, the fading works much
slower, especially if you don't have a very fast machine. The fading
speed also depends on the exact image you feed to it. Anyway enjoy ppl.
