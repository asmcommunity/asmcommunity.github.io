The past participle can be either awoke or awaked. However, like most languages there are words that are no longer used, kind of like deprecated APIs in Windows. Awaked is one of those, it is mainly found in poetry where metrical constraints call for a 2 syllable word. So in short, yes it is a correct conjugation of the verb however it would not normally be used it in casual conversation.
