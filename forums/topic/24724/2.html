Educational App
The programm accepts both awoke and awaked as correct answer to 3rd form.
Subject of the programm is to test knowledge or irregular English verbs according to the list from Oxford University. If you trust Oxford on the subject - you can trust the programm to test you knowlege. It's not me who tests you. I just made the test virtual. If there are multiple choises of right forms- the prog. acceptes any of them.
It includes both modern and old forms. If your form hasn't been accepted - it means that from Oxford's point of view the form isn't correct either in old or in modern usage. I'm not to decide what is right or wrong in English, it's Oxford jugement.
