1) First target: learn the 2000 most-important words. To learn them:
- Study the 500 basic words found in any small text-book about English, in your language
- Learn a little grammar from there
- buy http://eltcatalogue.pearsoned-ema.com/Course.asp?Callingpage=Catalogue&CourseID=GM  (it's completely in English!)
- Learn the 2000 words, shown in the last 6 pages there
2) Learn basic grammar
- buy http://www.amazon.com/Grammar-Use-Intermediate-answers-Self-study/dp/052162598X (it's completely in English!)
- solve all exercises
- meanwhile study all unknown words you found
3) Start reading or translating English literature (technical, classical, fiction). At least 5 books or 2000 pages.
Done, you now know what to do afterwards.
