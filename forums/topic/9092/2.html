PUSH & POP or 2 MOV
Personally, although the push/pop is not optimal, i like to use it when dealing with structures or variables with long names, i.e.: 



	assume	esi		:ptr <some struct>

	invoke	GetParentWindow,	hWNd

	push	eax

	pop	esi.member


In cases like this, i find that by using instructions that only require one operand it makes the source tidier and easier to read, therefore less likely to have bugs.
