HOWTO: random numbers
Thanks f0dder for going to all this effort, one thing not many out there may be aware of is that Agner Fogs Random Number routines have all been collected into a library.

Just download the ramdoma.zip for his site, place the libs in your masm lib folder and then with this include file you can use all his routines with simple invokes.
