Whats the use of registery
There are two benefits I know of as far as the registry (correct me if the second benefit is wrong) 1. You have a centralized database. I think it's nice that thanks to the registry, we don't have as many programs cluttering up our systems with various INI files. 2. Win32 has a faster method for searching the registry than it does for INI files. With INI files, Windows will scan from top to bottom in a linear fashion. Registry searches use some other scanning method. ---------------------------- Team2k PC development team: http://ppilot.homepage.com
