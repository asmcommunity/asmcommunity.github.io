BTW, I just remembered that when using dialog boxes, you should stay out of WM_USER messages under WM_USER + 100h or so... this is because WM_USER, WM_USER+1 and WM_USER+2 (and who knows how many other) are used by DM_GETDEFID, DM_SETDEFID and DM_REPOSITION messages.
