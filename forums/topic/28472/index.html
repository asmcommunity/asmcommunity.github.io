Hi disease_2000, to attach the extended style you do just this: 

INVOKE CreateWindowEx,WS_EX_CLIENTEDGE,offset class_SysListView32,0,LVS_SINGLESEL or LVS_REPORT or WS_CHILD or WS_VISIBLE,4,4,437,400,hWin,0,hInstance,0
MOV hListviewHandle,EAX
MOV EAX, LVS_EX_FLATSB
INVOKE SendMessage,hListviewHandle,LVM_SETEXTENDEDLISTVIEWSTYLE,0,EAX
That's it, you can look up the different styles on MSDN Stefan This message was edited by Stefan Krause, on 6/25/2001 4:58:19 PM
