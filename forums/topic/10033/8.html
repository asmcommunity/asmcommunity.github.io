Hi cakmak

Only F1 is taken by msdnhelp, you can still set up:

Shift+F1
Ctrl+F1
Shift+Ctrl+F1

To work with other help files.

Just change the following in RadASM.ini


;F1-Key=HelpFile
F1=$H\Win32.hlp
CF1=$H\x86eas.hlp
SF1=$H\Masm32.hlp
CSF1=$H\Rc.hlp
