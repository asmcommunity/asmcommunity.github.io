.686
.MODEL FLAT, STDCALL

REC1 RECORD last:1, third:1, second:1, first:1

STRC STRUCT
 Rec1 REC1 <>
STRC ENDS

.DATA?
Struct1 STRC <>
Struct2 STRC <>

.CODE
Start:
assume eax:ptr STRC
 mov	.Rec1, MASK first
 or	, MASK last
END Start


There are also some nice examples in the old MASM documentation.
