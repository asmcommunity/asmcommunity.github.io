Re: 8086 AD bus direction
8086 has pin 33 - mn/mx -> minimal mode (no bus controller) or maximal mode (with bus controller). in minimal mode pins st0-st2 means "DEN" (data enabled), "DT/R" (data transmit/receive = direction) and "M/IO" (memory/IO ports). when 8288 present maximal mode used, then those signals generated by 8288 bus controller, and st0-st2 used to control 8288 by cpu.
