thoughts on open source
Assembly programming is my hobby, I do not expect to make any money off it. I do it for the personal achievment and knowedge I can gain. So... I like to 'publish' some of what I do on my web page. You may call that 'open source' as I freely invite people to learn from what I've done. I don't do GNU or that. Should someone wish to SELL something derivative from my work, then I do expect to be paid. Thus I copyright everything I do to limit it to 'educational' use. I don't get fancy in the copyright disclaimer. Should my work wander into a commercial work, I'm not about to hunt someone down and sue. Should honest people re-ise my work, they would contact me.
