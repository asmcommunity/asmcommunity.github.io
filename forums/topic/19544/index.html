Embedded (Cellular-Phone) Assembly
If you do get a PalmOS based phone, you'll be interested in these three links:
Pila 2.0 - http://www.schaeckermann.net/pila-user-manual.html#direct_union
PilrRC - http://pilrc.sourceforge.net/
South Debugger - http://www.jensbruhn.de/wordpress/index.php?cat=5
It's a GUI java application that needs POSE, distributed by PalmOS people.

Pila does 68k, but at least with the PalmOS handhelds, the longtime CPU was 68k based and recently they've made the switch to ARM. I assume that PalmOS for the cellphones also needs to support 68k.
