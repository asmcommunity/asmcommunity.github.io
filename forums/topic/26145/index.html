Hi As far as I know, X-Calibre has made a fast GIF decoder, which he states is faster than UPX. I have never used, so I cannot tell you the quality of it. I think you can find promethee's JPEGLib on http://www.programmersheaven.com/. I doubt X-Calibre has made his GIF loader public, so you will have to ask him yourself. comrade
