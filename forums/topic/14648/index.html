I have a couple of corrections/additions:

1. Functions lstrcat, lstrcmp,lstrcmpi,lstrcpy,lstrcpyn,lstrlen are without 'A' in their import names (i.e. __imp__lstrlenA@4, __imp__lstrcatA@8....), also their Unicode counterparts are not listed.

2. Constants IMAGE_ARCHIVE_START := "!<arch>" #$d #$a;
IMAGE_ARCHIVE_END := "`" #$d #$a;
IMAGE_ARCHIVE_PAD := #$d #$a;

Correct forms: IMAGE_ARCHIVE_START := "!<arch>" #$a;
IMAGE_ARCHIVE_END := "`" #$a;
IMAGE_ARCHIVE_PAD := #$a;

3. Function wsprintf not included.
