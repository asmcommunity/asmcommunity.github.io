Why does MASM32lib''s memfill not use ''rep stos(?)'' ?
nyam, The answer is simple, the unrolled loop in memfill is a lot faster than REP STOS(?). This will vary some from one processor to another but generally the older string instructions are not internally well optimised in later Intel processors. Regards, hutch@pbq.com.au
