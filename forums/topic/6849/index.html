change the desktop colour?
ok, sorry everybody, found what i was looking for,

use

lea esi,buff
mov eax,COLOR_BACKGROUND
mov ,eax
mov eax,COL
mov ,eax
invoke SetSysColors, 1,ADDR buff, ADDR buff+4

to do the trick.
