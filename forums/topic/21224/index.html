Quote from readme:
AACircuit (Andy?s ASCII Circuit) has been developed to meet the need for posting schematics on text newsgroups. Formerly, schematics had to be posted to *.binaries NG?s or uploaded to a webserver and linked. Some people also laboriously drew the schematic with individual ASCII characters. While reading de.sci.electronics, I had the idea of developing a little tool to make the design of ASCII-circuits faster and simpler. So ASCIIPaint was born (first release 2001). Since this time the program has been enhanced due to the help and ideas of many users.
