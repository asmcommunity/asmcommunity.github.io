"Transparent" OpenGL on top of the desktop (sprite
Just a funny (but slow - although the sprite routine can certainly be optimized) idea: mix a sprite routine with OpenGL, and so you have a 3D object without window or background, floating on top of your desktop, that can be animated. Zip includes full source (Tasm32) and two binaries (one with a smaller object for slow computers). You can get pure animated 2D sprite or pure 3D OpenGL demos in 29A virus mag #6.

Use the left mouse button to rotate, right to zoom (move the mouse slowly, it's sensitive), space to flat-shade one half of the object.
