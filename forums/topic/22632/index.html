Google sucks
Am I the only one who noticed that lately?

I loved Google but in the last couple of weeks I can't seem to find anything anymore. Looking for local stores has become an impossible task, looking up phone numbers and addresses just as well. I used to be able just to type the store name and it's state and it presented me with ratings, addresses, phone numbers and more. All relevant. Now, I believe according to Google there are no Hard Wood Floor places anywhere in the US leave alone CT.
Also I seem to be getting hammered with crap when searching for stuff. I mean, before you used to be able to hint at something and more often than not get the page you were actually looking for, now I find myself scrolling up to page 8+ before finding anything or giving up.

I caught myself lately to give msn search a try and am thinking to seriously being in need for a new search engine. Google lost it and I'm pissed. Instead of creating more and more beta crap they should try to keep their flagship going.

Of course this rant only reflects my opinion. (And I still keep my gmail account)
