Hi jerrygao2001!

This one works for me:

1. DownLoad latest winio package (v. 2 available now fro XP too)
2. use this in your reg_read f-n: 



reg_read PROC



pusha

movzx ebx, bx

mov dat4write, ebx

invoke SetPortVal, 070h, dat4write, 2

invoke GetPortVal, 071h, addr szTemp, 2

popa



mov eax, szTemp

ret

reg_read ENDP

[\CODE]
