At Guard users beware!!!
Solution to disabling At Guard / Ad Blocking: Under Web, click Add Site and enter www.hiroshimator.com. Then (with the net entry selected) click the Add button under the Ad Blocking, select permit and enter "adit=" (with no quotes). That should fix the problem.
