If you are targeting the dreamcast, you'll need an SH4 assembler (the dreamcast isn't a plain SH4 either, it has extras that I don't know about, last time I looked at DC hardware was a year ago, and then it was still a closely guarded Sega secret)!
The linker can handle SH4 but I don't think ML will.
If you are planning on moving your app around WinCE devices I'd recomend C and try to stick to the ANSI stuff, most WinCE platforms will run on ARM or variants of it, but there are plenty of others out there too.
