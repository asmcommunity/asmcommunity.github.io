MSGSTRUCT in win32.inc (TASM users)
The MSGSTRUCT structure in win32.inc supplied with TASM is declared incorrectly. The last member msPT should be POINT <> which is 2 double words not one.

Just wanted to let everyone know so they don't find out the hard way as I did :rolleyes:
