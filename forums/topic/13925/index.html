DirectDraw and chat
This part of game is not going to bee too slow anyway :P

You could use GetDc method on a surface and the use standard Win API to draw the text (TextOut or something) on the surface and at last Draw the surface on backbuffer or primary surface. This is the easy and somehow slow method.

For superfast method : cache/paint your font(s) in a bitmap then load this bitmap into an surface.

Then use DirectDraw's native Blt Method to put chars into backbuffer/primary surface == this is the slightly faster method.
