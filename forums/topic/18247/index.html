qwords and floats to ASCII
The attached zip contains 3 procedures.

Two of them are simplified algos for converting floats to null-terminating strings with restrictions: the fl2a9 allows a maximum of 9 significant (integer+decimal) digits while the fl2a18 allows a maximum of 18.

Although the fl2a18 could be used also for converting most qwords to ascii, it wouldn't cover the entire range. The algo was thus modified for the qw2a procedure so that the entire range of signed qwords (+/-9223372036854775807) would be covered.
