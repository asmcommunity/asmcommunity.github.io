Never mind, I have investigated with SysInternals' RegMon.

The keys seem to be here:
HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Explorer\UserAssist

What is weird about it, is that entries for the menu items are encrypted with ROT13 (only the letters, the symbols and numbers stay intact)
