Adobe Flash for 64-bit Firefox
Just downloaded it and installed it.  Works beautiful even for an alpha version.

Download it at http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/

Simple instructions for Fedora 14 64-bit users users:


cd ~/Downloads
gunzip flashplayer10_2_p3_64bit_linux_111710.tar.gz
tar xvf flashplayer10_2_p3_64bit_linux_111710.tar
cp libflashplayer.so /usr/lib64/mozilla/plugins
chmod 755 /usr/lib64/mozilla/plugins/libflashplayer.so


enjoy :)
