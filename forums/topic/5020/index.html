Dicom 3
DICOM means Digital Imaging and Communication in Medicine. It's a standard in medical imaging. It specifies how to store and exchange medical images. I have studied a part of the protocol in a school project. There is a lot of information at this site : http://www.dclunie.com/
Good luck!
