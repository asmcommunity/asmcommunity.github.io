RadAsm linking
Hi minor28

The ',' confuses RadASM.
Replace it with '|' like this:

7,O,$B\LINK.EXE /SECTION:.bss|S /DLL /DEF:$6 /SUBSYSTEM:WINDOWS /LIBPATH:"$L",3

The '|' will be replaced with a ',' by RadASM in the final parsing.
