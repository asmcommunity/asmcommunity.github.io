Grid in SysListView
Hi,

You can use LVM_SETEXTENDEDLISTVIEWSTYLE with LVS_EX_GRIDLINES for grid lines. 





	call SendMessageA, lbox, LVM_SETEXTENDEDLISTVIEWSTYLE, 0,\

			   LVS_EX_FULLROWSELECT or \

			   LVS_EX_HEADERDRAGDROP or \

			  [b] LVS_EX_GRIDLINES[/b]  + LVS_EX_SUBITEMIMAGES + LVS_EX_CHECKBOXES +\

			   LVS_EX_HEADERDRAGDROP
