strange error
The result of a division is always in eax:edx. eax being the whole numbers and edx the remainder. Before a division you need to clear edx and therefore you can't use it to divide eax with it.

Use this instead:

invoke SendMessage.....
xor edx,edx
mov ecx,60
idiv ecx
