ObjAsm32 RadASM Template
I finally got around to building a template for RadASM that will suport the ObjASM32 model (v1.2).

Download and read the attached README.TXT.

This template will get you the basic starting point Window Application (Class), with an additional About Dialog that is customizable to represent you.

You must have ObjAsm32 installed in your Masm32 directory, and follow the update note in the readme, as one of the classes was upgraded to suport RadASM (DialogAbout.inc).

Hope you like it and can use this,
