MyMatrix STRUCT

	UNION

		f REAL4 16 dup (?)

		STRUCT

			w REAL 4 dup (?)

			x REAL 4 dup (?)

			y REAL 4 dup (?)

			z REAL 4 dup (?)

		ENDS

		STRUCT

			w0 REAL4 ?

			w1 REAL4 ?

			w2 REAL4 ?

			w3 REAL4 ?

			x0 REAL4 ?

			x1 REAL4 ?

			x2 REAL4 ?

			x3 REAL4 ?

			y0 REAL4 ?

			y1 REAL4 ?

			y2 REAL4 ?

			y3 REAL4 ?

			z0 REAL4 ?

			z1 REAL4 ?

			z2 REAL4 ?

			z3 REAL4 ?

		ENDS
