Cool processor...
If anyone is thinking of robotics.. you should check out this new processor.

IsoMax

Its got to many kool features to talk about. Kinda pricy but very cool chip. Its got internal parallel engins to drive your code. You can even program (ie write code) in real time, while other parallel processes are running existing code!

It also has an impressive math abilty (which include trig). Chalk full of I/O, A/D, PWM's and more.

I was surprised to even see the Open CAN potocol being suported on it! (This is a very good network protocol for hardware devices).

Simply COOL :alright:
