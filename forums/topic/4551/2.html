ResEdit Update (03-31-2002)
To smurf:

Each Groupbox on ResEdit is a parent for it's
controls, normally after you create the Groupbox
(You can draw it to the proper size and position)
you draw the controls within it's boundries.

That's why I programed it the way I did.

It's no big deal to click on the move Groupbox
checkbox to move the Groupbox.

The main reason I made the Groupbox the parent of
it's controls, is when you move the Groupbox the
controls go with it.
