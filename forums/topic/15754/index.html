Issues with my screensaver...
Just to add a concluding note to anyone that is currious... I think i finally figured it out. In your Screen Saver tab, under Energy Star settings, there is a drop box for "System Standby" timer. While I have the HD's and monitor set up, i moved this catagory to NEVER. After many trials, it have yet to see it lock up again. So I can only assume it was this option that was failing to restart... even from the power button.
