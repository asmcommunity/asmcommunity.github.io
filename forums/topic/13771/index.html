GetOpenFileName won't work ?
You need to fill the ofn struct up first.

Like: 
ofn OPENFILENAME <sizeof OPENFILENAME,0,400000h,OFFSET FileFilter,0,0,0,OFFSET FileName,\

				256,0,0,0,0,\

				OFN_FILEMUSTEXIST or OFN_PATHMUSTEXIST or OFN_LONGNAMES or OFN_EXPLORER or OFN_HIDEREADONLY,\

				0,0,0,0,0,0>
