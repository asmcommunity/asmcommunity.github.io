From direct experience gathered at my last job, working with USB at both the hardware and system level requires a lot of work (both in the research phase and the development phase) to get things right.

A short cut that you might want to investigate is doing some form of USB to serial / serial to USB.  Since the adapter cable would come with the needed interface driver you could just code as if you were writing to a serial port rather than messing around with URB's
