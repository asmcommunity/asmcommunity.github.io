Drakken,

I had that problem. To write in a remote process, you
must change the access protection on the region of
pages that you want to write.

You can do it calling to VirtualProtectEx before
call WriteProcessMemory. Then you have to
restaure the original acces protection calling
again to VirtualProtectEx.
