Wallpapering a Window
Several ways. If the bitmap is small (8x8 pixles) under W95, just assign the bitmap's handle to the window background brush. W98 and above remove the size limitation. This will also tile a bitmap smaller then the window. The other way is to activly blit the bitmap from a temporary compatable dc to the window in the WM_PAINT message handler. This allows you fuller control over where the bitmap appears. That's the outline, ask again if you need more info on this. E
