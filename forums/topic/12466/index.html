ArcTangent=Tan^-1?
Ah. The two are actually the same. The inverse tan function, also called arctan(x), is usually denoted by tan^-1(x). The notation may seem a little funny though since tan^-1(x) (arctan(x)) is not tan(x)^-1 (1 / tan(x)) :)

Hope that clears it up.

ArcTangent=Tan^-1?
tan(arctan(x))=x (moved into the first or fourth quadrant)

arctan(x) can also be notated as tan^-1(x)... this is not to be confused with tan(x^-1) or tan(x)^-1.

tan(x^-1) is the same as tan(y) where y=x^-1.
tan(x)^-1 is the same as y^-1 where y=tan(x)
