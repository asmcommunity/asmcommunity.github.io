But when I go to either of my Limited User accounts, there are no icons on the desktop.

Re: Icons misssing on 2 limited user accounts
Use your Admin account and go to C:\Documents and Settings\...limited user name...\Desktop\ and check if there are icons. If so you can back them up. You could also copy them from where they are and add them to C:\Documents and Settings\All Users\Desktop\ and  it adds them to All Users.
