Re: Program's execution speed benchmark
Get yourself a profiler :)

AMD has CodeAnalyst iirc, which is (or at least used to be) free.
Intel has VTune, which kicks some serious behind.
Visual Studio 6 has a profiler, and I believe the "ouch this is so expensive it's going to cost me my soul" versions of Visual Studio 2005 has profiling, too.

Profilers are good in that they allow you to locate the bottlenecks in your program... you can do that manually of course, and then do stuff like GetTickCount or RDTSC to profile them for speed.
