Custom Tab Control
Heya Guys,

Here I contribute a 'kindof' TAB control as the Tab control windows gives us - really blows - if you ever played with them before you know what I mean.

This Tab Control has automatic Tooltips, can overlay an Editfield for renaming purposes, is easily extendable using 10 reserved Menuitem identifiers for an integrated PopupMenu. In addition to that it's can be in any color you want. And much much more.

Attached to this thread is the following:

Tab060807.zip : contains Tab.lib, Tab.inc, Tab.chm (helpfile)
Tabsample060807.zip: A sample application showing off the TabControls features.
