Hi ssa

Try changing the line:

3=7,O,$B\Link /SECTION:.bss|S /DLL /DEF:*.def /SUBSYSTEM:WINDOWS /LIBPATH:"$L",3

to:

3=7,O,$B\Link /SECTION:.bss|S /DLL /DEF:$6 /SUBSYSTEM:WINDOWS /LIBPATH:"$L",3

$6 is replaced by redasm to 'yourdll.def'
