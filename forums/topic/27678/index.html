modest proposal
Clarification: The messageboard will stay of course and should have the same functionality as you're used to: Private messageboards will still exist (bet you didn't knew we had some right now :D) Container forums like the 'All' forum will stay too, since they have proved their worth. Topics will have a 'must see' rating (those of you that visit arstechnica know what I mean) indicating when a topic is really interesting and thus a 'must see' Polls will stay, but I'll try to integrate both check as radio buttons, so multiple values can be checked at once. Only registered members can vote, just like now. Projects: Possibility to upload and delete files on a registered user basis. Each project gets it's own messageboard, the project creator is the moderator on the forum. These forums are private and can only be visited by members registered to the project. Only active projectmembers can view/download the files, others can only look at the project's status. Tutorials/Articles: Possibility to upload tutorials and divide them into categories. Everey tutorial gets a public forum. News: News can be uploaded but I'm not sure by whom right now. Every news item can be commented on (like slashdot). Code-base: files and source can be uploaded. Divided into categories. Everycode piece should get it's forum or possibility to comment. Revision numbers and multiple file versions should be incorporated as well. Members: The usual thing. Members will get their own little space, listing their files, articles,projects,... They'll get favorites and whatever else we can think of. Private messages will also be available inter members (ability to block specific members from sending you messages) Some things are still uncertain (I have to look at my possibilities on the new space) but will get clearer in the future. I post this so that you can get a clearer view in the monstrosity that I've derived from all your suggestions :D comment, comment, comment :)
