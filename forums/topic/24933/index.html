InstallDll proc hWin:DWORD,fOpt:DWORD
Description:
This function is called depending on what functions are monitored.
The DLL can perform tasks based on values passed to it.
Parameters:
hWin: is a handle to the RadASM main window,
you will send messages to this window in
order to extract the information you need for your AddIn
fOpt: is the DWORD parameter that was specified in the RadASM.ini
entry for your AddIn
