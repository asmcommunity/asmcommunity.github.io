detecting negative numbers
AFAIK, "test" should be equal to "or" in the terms of performance. But the "test" instruction is more clear in the terms of readability, because it make exactly the operation it is intended to make independently of the context of arguments, thus the reader should not check the arguments to determine whether this is simple check for the sign or zero, or is really "or" operator that make some masking on the operands.
If the instruction is "test", in most cases the one should only check the instruction after the "test" to determine what actually happen.
