How to detect version of DirectX?
And what's about 


HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\DirectX


?

Just open this key, and query the value of "InstalledVersion".
On my machine here it's "00 00 00 08 00 00 00 01" (8.1, REG_BINARY. )
