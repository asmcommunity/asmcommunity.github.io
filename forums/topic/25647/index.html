Re: why isnt MASM working on win xp?
MASM sourcecode typically begins with three compiler directives:

.486 ;or some other minimum cpu opcode set
.model tiny ;for DOS apps.. flat,stdcall for win32 apps
option casemap:none ; capital letters matter to us


and then the rest of your sourcecode...
