8-bit sample -> 16-bit sample
Use 8bit value as hi-part of 16bit value.

In C language:

short var16 = ((short)var8) << 8;

In ASM:

mov al,
shl ax,8
mov ,ax

or if u know al value is zero:

mov ah,
mov ,ax
