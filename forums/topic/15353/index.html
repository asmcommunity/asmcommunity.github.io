structure global declaration
Be sure that the structure VARIANT is not defined with predetermined values, i.e. 
VARIANT struct

	feild1 dd 777

	feild2 dd 123

VARIANT ends
Or alternatively insert a ? for every one of the fields:

varnt VARIANT <?,?>
