What does IMUL AX,SI,CE6D do ?
Hi Zeux. IMUL AX,SI,CE6D is Immediate 16-Bit Multiplication. SI is multiplied by 0CE6Dh with the product put into AX. Both the destination (AX) and multiplicand (SI) must be 16-bit numbers for this particular operation.
