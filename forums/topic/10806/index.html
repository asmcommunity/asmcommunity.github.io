Look into:
RegNotifyChangeKeyValue

The SHChange* apis are only available on W2K and above and will do what you want and more... Will let you know just about anything that happens with the OS.
SHChangeNotifyRegister
SHChangeNotifyDeregister
SHChangeNotification_Lock
SHChangeNotification_Unlock

I think there was 1 or 2 more and or an callback API that worked on win9.x but can't remember now...
