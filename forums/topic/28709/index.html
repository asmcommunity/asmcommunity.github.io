Solution to IE7 opening Firefox
Having issues with Internet Explorer opening your default browser (such as Firefox) instead of showing pages itself???

Solution
-Close Internet Explorer if open
-Rename/delete "ieproxy.dll" found in your Internet Explorer directory.
-Delete the Registry entry HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\CLSID\{c90250f3-4d7d-4991-9b69-a5c5bc1c2ae6}
-Open Internet Explorer and enjoy :)
