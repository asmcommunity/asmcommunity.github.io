As for your project, it's a major undertaking. You'll really need to hook into DirectDraw in order to support most cards, you'll probably need a keyboard driver, etc. Try googling for "crudeice", perhaps wkr will be willing to chat with you if you have problems.

Also note that this board isn't the most suitable place to talk about this issue - you WILL need to do some kernel reverse engineering, and even though it's "probably legal" to do this since you're doing it for interop purposes, the lines are grey and we want to avoid problems here.
