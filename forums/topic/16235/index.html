What am I doing wrong?
are RC.EXE and ML.EXE somewhere in your search path? The usual place for it is c:\masm32\bin (may vary on your system)

If you haven't yet, you need to install masm32 or if you have already installed it, go to the options menu in RADasm and choose the "set paths" menu and make sure the paths point to the proper binaries.

You may also need to set up environment paths to recognize the location of masm32 bins and libs.
