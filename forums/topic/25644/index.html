Re: LaTeX
Depends on your needs.

It can be a real pain if you don't have some setup template, and if you're used to writing in word with "change font" instead of typographic styles, you have a lot of pain to go through.

But if you need to write where the content is important and not framemaker-style layout, especially if you need lots of math equations, it's a very powerful tool. And you can get very good-looking stuff with it.
