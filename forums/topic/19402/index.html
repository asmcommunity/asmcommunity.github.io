bitmap brushes and text
You'll need one or two additional DCs,
make DC1 all black, and print text on it with white textcolor. Then, use SRCAND to combine the bitmap and text over DC2 - the black areas will stay, white will be filled with the bitmap. Then, using DC1 as mask, BitBlt over the destination DC (use the trick to blt transparent bitmaps)
