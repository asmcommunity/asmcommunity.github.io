Fasm Dialog Example, COFF format
With the new coff output Fasm for me at least has finally taken over from Masm.

I've written a small Dialog shell, I always start a new project by going back to something like this. It may be of use to someone. :)

Move the include folder to your Fasm folder, also it assumes masm32 is installed on the same drive (it runs Link from there)

This may not be the ideal method, for example including all the externals results in a obj file over 60kb but it also makes programming easier. Compilation time seem slightly faster then Masm, but I didn't test it too much, its fast enough for me not to care further :cool:
