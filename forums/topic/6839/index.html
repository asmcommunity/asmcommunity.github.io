subclassing button question
I think the button will capture the mouse on WM_LBUTTONDOWN.
I found a good way of handling this is: 



@@LButtonUp:

    movzx eax,[lParam]

    cmp eax,BUTTONWIDTH

    ja @@NotInButton

    movzx eax,[lParam+2]

    cmp eax,BUTTONHEIGHT

    ja @@NotInButton

@@InButton:

    ...

    ret

@@NotInButton:

    ret
