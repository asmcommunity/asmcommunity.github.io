WinME and CreateStatusWindow ?
You have declared sbParts incorrectly. It should be an array
of DWORDS.

EXAMPLE: 


LOCAL sbParts[4]:DWORD



mov    [sbParts +  0],80

mov    [sbParts +  4],140

mov    [sbParts +  8],-1



invoke SendMessage,hStatusBar,SB_SETPARTS,3,addr sbParts
