Low Level Discussion
With this sub-forum, we are trying to eliminate the misconception that the discussion of low level concepts are forbidden. In fact, the opposite is true!!! We'd love for everyone to participate in low level discussion, as such subjects really do go hand-in-hand with what Assembly Language is all about.

We actively try to avoid drawing in malicious types and shady characters, and this often conflicts with allowing the disucssion of gray-area topics that most low level concepts fall in to. This sub-forum is a compromise in favor of allowing low level discussion to thrive.

While the Community Rules still apply, this sub-forum should be considered more "relaxed" and a good place to discuss legal subjects that may have previously been thought of as taboo.

Thanks for your attention. -SpooK
