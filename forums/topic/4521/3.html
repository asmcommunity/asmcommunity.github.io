Hi JimmyClif

My best guess is that there is something wrong with your ini files, RadASM.ini and masm.ini.
In most cases section in RadASM.ini is the cause.

Chech these values:

;ToolWindow=
;Visible, (TRUE/FALSE)
;Docked, (TRUE/FALSE)
;Pos, (Left=1,Top=2,Right=3,Bottom=4)
;IsChildOf,
;DockedWidth,
;DockedHeight,
;FloatingRECT (Left,Top,Width,Height)
Project=1,1,3,0,177,307,7,187,143,416
Output=0,1,4,0,142,100,0,0,27,27
ToolBox=1,1,1,0,54,98,333,185,419,417
Property=1,1,4,1,184,400,505,64,902,296
TabTool=1,1,2,0,28,41,373,113,739,161
;The last tool will be clipped by all others
;1=Project, 2=Output, 3=ToolBox, 4=Property, 5=TabTool
ToolClipping=1,5,3,4,2

If you cannot figure it out, post your ini file and I will check them out for you.
