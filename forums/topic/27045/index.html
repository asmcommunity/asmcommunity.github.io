Resource''s in LIBS?
I believe you can just add the resource compiled object .obj into the lib link statement. Something like this: \masm32\bin\ml /nologo /c /coff *.asm \masm32\bin\rc /v rsrc.rc \masm32\bin\cvtres /machine:ix86 rsrc.res \masm32\bin\lib *.obj /out:MyLib.lib And welcome to the board :-)
