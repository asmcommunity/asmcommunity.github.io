In order to use the 3DNow! instructions, you have to use the CPU switch .K3D (which switch should also give you MMX instructions). As for Intel, I don't believe they'd ever follow AMD in anything...AMD is the second company so far as they're concerned. :) What I heard them say was they would implement instructions similar in nature to AMD's 3DNow!, which can be found in the Pentium III's SSE, and the Pentium 4's SSE2 instructions. However, they're not opcode compatible.
