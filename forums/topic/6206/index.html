WS_CHILD or WS_VISIBLE

or

WS_CHILD + WS_VISIBLE



In this case the 'OR' operator or the '+' (add) operator perform exactly the same job because WS_CHILD and WS_VISIBLE are flags with only different bits set.

e.g. 


FLAG1 EQU 001b

FLAG2 EQU 010b



'FLAG1 OR FLAG2':

01

10 OR

11



'FLAG1 + FLAG2':

01 = 1

10 = 2

1 + 2 = 3

3 = 11 (In binary)


However, if say FLAG2 EQU 011b then the operators obviosly wouldn't perform equal operations.
