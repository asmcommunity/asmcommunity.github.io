RAW Sockets in Win200 & NT
Check out the functions available through Winsock.dll. This is a Microsoft dll, but it is their version of the Berkeley sockets standard (and as you are operating on Windoze, you will never get fully away from MS).
I understand that it is reasonably close to the Unix version, although i have never done sockets on Unix,so i can't say exactly how close it is, but it would be an easy port.

Also, (and there is a small possibility i am wrong here), you cannot do raw sockets on NT4 unless you write your own kernel mode driver.
