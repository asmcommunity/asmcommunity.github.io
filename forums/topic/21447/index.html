ofn   OPENFILENAME <>

declares ofn as an OPENFILENAME structure (look in windows.inc or the PlatformSDK to see what this structure is). You can "mov eax, sizeof ofn" to see the size of the variable. Try comparing the size of the original declaration to "ofn dd ?".
