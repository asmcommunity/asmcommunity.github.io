binary fun...
if we write the bit count for numbers in increasing order:
dec biny #-->

__0 0000 0 000

__1 0001 1 001

__2 0010 1 001

__3 0011 2 010

__4 0100 1 001

__5 0101 2 010

__6 0110 2 010

__7 0111 3 011

__8 1000 1 001

__9 1001 2 010

_10 1010 2 010

_11 1011 3 011

_12 1100 2 010

_13 1101 3 011

_14 1110 3 011

_15 1111 4 100
...we can note the least significant bit of the bit count follow a self similar pattern...

01 ; start
01 ; generator
0110
01101001
0110100110010110

...it is fractal. :-D
