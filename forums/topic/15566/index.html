taper ratio on spiral
There is another taper ratio called Rational BSPLINE where the Radius is simply linear interpolated over the Length of the spiral (cone).
Theres another called non rational KSPLINE where the Radius is spherically interpolated over the Length, this can be easy or hard, you can use easy sine function to calc current R or u can do it hard but more accurate using quaternion spherical lerping (aka slerping) :)
