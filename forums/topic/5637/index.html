I have a very stupid question... RadASM code editor has a selection bar (a thin red strip) on its left side. How can I do the same in RichEdit control? Which style or message must I use?

Selection bar for RichEdit control
Vladimir,

invoke SendMessage,hRichEd,EM_SETOPTIONS,ECOOP_XOR,ECO_SELECTIONBAR

Its a toggle that you can call multiple times and it swaps back and forth.
